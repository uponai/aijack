PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. General Information (GDPR Compliance)

AIJACK ("we", "us", or "our") operates this website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we are the Data Controller of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: We collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service ("Log Data"), including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Unsolicited Data: Any search queries or stack data you create is stored to provide the relevant service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features (e.g., saving stacks, rating tools).
  • To provide customer support.
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service.

4. Data Retention & Security

We recognize the importance of information security and act in accordance with applicable laws to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights, including:

  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent.
