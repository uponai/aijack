How it works

Unlike traditional search that looks for exact keywords, our Semantic Core understands the *meaning* and *intent* behind your words. It matches your problem to the solution description, not just the name.

Don't just search:
"Email marketing tool"
Instead, describe it:
"I need to automate cold outreach emails to specialized clients"

Try asking things like:

  • "Tools to help me design logos faster using AI"
  • "Analyze large PDF documents for legal contracts"
  • "Robots that can handle warehouse logistics"
